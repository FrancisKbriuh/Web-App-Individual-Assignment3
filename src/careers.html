Personal Trainer

We are Looking for talented and experienced Personal Trainer's to help us get our members up and running in their own fitness careers or journey.
As a Personal Trainer you will be tasked to work with fellow members in there fitness journey showing the basic needs to get started.
You will be building a bond with our team member learning more about them and their daily lives to further chart what they need getting started.
For example, their diet, their working environment, their age, how much are they moving per day, and what their day of life looks like, etc.
You will also be at our front desk making sure people are getting checked in managing the flow of members going in and out when not training your clients.
Lastly, with this opportunity, you will be able to build your clientele for the future so that our members will be able to reference you to others.

Requirements

Be of 18 years of age or older
GED
Pass accredited training exam
Problem-Solver
Previous Experience in Personal Training
Communication Skills
